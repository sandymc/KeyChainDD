Uninstalling KeyChainDD

There is only one step required to uninstall KeyChainDD.

To uninstall KeyChainDD do the following:

Drag KeyChainDD.app from the Applications folder to the Trash

Drag KeyChainDD.app from the Applications folder (or wherever else you put it originally) to the Trash

Once you have dragged KeyChainDD to the Trash, it is uninstalled.
